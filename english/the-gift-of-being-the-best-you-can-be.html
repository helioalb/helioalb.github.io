The Gift of Being the Best you can Be

Sometimes you

Think that you

Need to be perfect

That you cannot

Make mistakes

At these times

You put so much

Pressure on yourself

I wish that you

Would realize

That you are

a human being

Like everyone else

Capable of

Reaching great potential

but not capable of

being perfect

So please

Just do your best

and realize that

this is enough

Don't compare yourself

to anyone

Be happy to be

The wonderful,

unique, very special

person that you are
